welcome to Hotel Rara Prince

Hotel Rara Prince – Comfort & Convenience Near New Buspark Located near the vibrant Naya Buspark, Hotel Rara Prince has been offering warm hospitality for over 6 to 7 years. With comfortable rooms equipped with flat-screen TVs, satellite channels, and essential amenities like free toiletries and hot showers, it's an ideal stop for both business and leisure travelers.
Guests can relax at the in-house bar or enjoy delicious meals at the restaurant serving local and international cuisine. Its prime location and cozy atmosphere make Hotel Rara Prince a trusted choice for a pleasant stay in Kathmandu.
With years of experience in serving guests from all walks of life, Hotel Rara Prince takes pride in its friendly staff and personalized service. Whether you're catching an early bus, exploring the city, or simply looking for a peaceful retreat, the hotel offers a welcoming environment that feels just like home.

The Soul Behind Rara Prince

Hotel Rara Prince is proudly owned and managed by Bhim Bahadur Khatri , whose dedication and vision have shaped the hotel into a welcoming haven for travelers. With a strong commitment to hospitality and guest satisfaction, Mr. Khatri brings years of experience and a personal touch to every aspect of the hotel’s operations. His hands-on approach and focus on quality service have earned the trust of countless guests over the years.

Beyond just running a hotel, Mr. Khatri believes in creating meaningful experiences for every guest who walks through the door. He is deeply rooted in the values of Nepali warmth and generosity, and this is reflected in the way the hotel is run—from the attentive staff to the cozy, homelike environment. Under his leadership, Hotel Rara Prince continues to grow not just as a business, but as a place where comfort, care, and community come together.

Sirjana Khatri, the financial investor behind Hotel Rara Prince, plays a vital role in the hotel's continued growth and success. Though she is based abroad, Sirjana remains deeply connected to the values and aspirations that define Hotel Rara Prince. Her investment is more than financial—it reflects a commitment to creating sustainable opportunities back home, empowering the local team, and enhancing the overall guest experience. Through her involvement, the hotel continues to thrive as a welcoming space where tradition, care, and modern hospitality come together.
